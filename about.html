Kirsten Groenewald

With two full-time artists as my parents, I've been immersed in the world of design and art since childhood. I pursued art throughout my school years and now, as a hobby, I express myself through painting and drawing, primarily using acrylics.

Over the course of 12 years of running my own business, I channeled my creativity as a valuable outlet. I handled website updates, photography, and the creation of social media content. I even designed my own advertisements using Canva.

More recently, I've delved into the world of Figma and have fallen in love with its versatility. I've discovered that my passion for creativity drives me to take on new challenges and projects, as I've been doing over the past few months. Additionally, I'm currently mastering CSS and HTML, and I'm steadily gaining proficiency in JavaScript.
